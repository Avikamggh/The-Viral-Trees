About SHE ORBITS

SHE ORBITS.

SHE ORBITS is a pioneering initiative dedicated to empowering girls aged 13 to 25 in the fields of space science, technology, engineering, and innovation. Founded by aerospace researcher Asmaa Hossam, we connect young women with world-class female leaders through workshops, mentorship, and real-world research experiences.

Our mission is to create a pipeline of global female talent equipped to take part in the future of space exploration. We aim to inspire, educate, and equip girls worldwide through hands-on workshops, mentorship, and real-world research experiences, ensuring they are not just observers but active participants in the space industry.

We envision a world where girls from every background have the opportunity to access the tools, role models, and confidence needed to reach the stars and lead humanity beyond Earth. Our team is passionate about fostering global female talent.
