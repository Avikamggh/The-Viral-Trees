Mission & Vision

01
Our Purpose

Our Mission

Empowering Girls to Reach for the Stars

SHE ORBITS is a pioneering non-profit organization dedicated to inspiring, educating, and equipping girls aged 13-25 worldwide through hands-on space workshops, mentorship programs, and real-world research experiences. We create a global pipeline of female talent prepared to lead humanity's journey beyond Earth, ensuring they are not just observers but active participants and innovators in the space industry.

02
Our Future

Our Vision

A Universe of Equal Opportunities

We envision a world where girls from every background have the opportunity to access the tools, role models, and confidence needed to reach the stars. Our vision is to create a future where women lead space exploration missions, drive aerospace innovation, and shape humanity's multi-planetary future. By 2030, we aim to have empowered 10,000 girls globally, creating a diverse network of female space leaders across all continents.

03
Our Values

Core Values That Drive Us

The principles that guide our organization's mission to empower girls in space and STEM fields.

Inclusion & Diversity

We embrace diversity and ensure equal opportunities for all girls, regardless of their background, ethnicity, or economic status.

Innovation

We foster creativity and encourage groundbreaking ideas that push the boundaries of what's possible in space exploration.

Empowerment

We empower girls to become confident leaders and innovators in STEM fields through mentorship and hands-on experiences.

Collaboration

We build strong networks and partnerships globally to create opportunities for girls in the space industry.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in education and training, providing world-class programs and resources.

Global Impact

We work to create lasting positive change in communities worldwide through our programs and initiatives.

04
Our Goals

Strategic Goals 2025-2030

Our roadmap to creating a more inclusive future in space exploration.

Expand Global Reach

Establish SHE ORBITS programs in 50+ countries, reaching underserved communities and creating equal opportunities for all girls interested in space.

Launch Satellite Mission

Design, build, and launch our first all-girls developed CubeSat mission by 2027, demonstrating the capabilities of our participants.

Create Mentorship Network

Build a global network of 500+ female space professionals mentoring the next generation of girls in aerospace careers.

Develop Online Academy

Launch a comprehensive online learning platform offering space education to girls worldwide, breaking geographical barriers.

Partner with Space Agencies

Establish formal partnerships with NASA, ESA, ISRO, and other space agencies for internships and research opportunities.

Scholarship Program

Provide 1,000+ scholarships annually for girls to pursue aerospace engineering and space science degrees globally.

Empowering girls globally to lead in space and STEM fields. We are a non-profit organization creating a pipeline of female talent equipped to take part in the future of space exploration through innovative workshops, mentorship, and real-world research experiences.
