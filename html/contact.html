Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs and how to get involved.

Our programs are designed for girls aged 13-25 from all backgrounds and countries. No prior space or STEM experience is required - just passion and curiosity! We offer different program levels from beginners to advanced participants.

Many of our programs are free or low-cost thanks to our sponsors and partners. We also offer full and partial scholarships based on need and merit. Financial constraints should never be a barrier to reaching for the stars!

Program lengths vary: Weekend workshops (2-3 days), Summer bootcamps (2-8 weeks), Online courses (4-12 weeks), Year-long fellowships, and ongoing mentorship programs. We design flexible options to fit different schedules and commitments.
