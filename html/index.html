Our Programs

Space Innovation Programs

Comprehensive aerospace programs designed to empower women with cutting-edge skills in satellite technology, mission planning, space research, and leadership in the global space industry.

  • Advanced training in satellite design, development, and deployment. Our all-women engineering teams work on real satellite missions from conception to launch, gaining hands-on experience in cutting-edge aerospace technology.

  • Comprehensive mission control operations training preparing women for leadership roles in space missions. Includes flight dynamics, orbital mechanics, and real-time decision-making in mission-critical scenarios.

  • State-of-the-art research facilities for astrobiology, planetary science, and space medicine. Women scientists lead groundbreaking research projects contributing to humanity's understanding of space and life beyond Earth.

  • Inspiring the next generation of women in STEM through school partnerships, mentorship programs, and space camps. We provide scholarships and hands-on experiences to young girls passionate about space exploration.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about our programs, admission requirements, and how SHE ORBITS is creating pathways for women to lead in the global space industry.

Our programs are open to women and girls from all backgrounds who are passionate about space and STEM. We offer programs for high school students, university students, and professionals looking to transition into aerospace careers. No prior space experience required - just curiosity and commitment!

Participants work on real space missions including satellite design and launch, Mars habitat development, asteroid mining research, and space medicine experiments. You'll have hands-on experience with mission control operations, data analysis from active satellites, and collaborative projects with international space agencies.

Yes! We offer full and partial scholarships based on merit and need. Over 60% of our participants receive some form of financial assistance. We also have partnerships with corporations and space agencies that sponsor talented women. Our goal is to ensure financial barriers don't prevent anyone from reaching the stars.

Our graduates work at leading space agencies (NASA, ESA, ISRO), private space companies (SpaceX, Blue Origin, Virgin Galactic), and research institutions worldwide. Career paths include satellite engineering, mission planning, space medicine, astrobiology, and space policy. With our 95% job placement rate, you'll be well-positioned for your dream space career.

Programs range from 6-week intensive bootcamps to 2-year comprehensive training programs. We offer both on-site programs at our state-of-the-art facilities and hybrid options combining online learning with periodic hands-on sessions. International participants can join our virtual labs and collaborate on global projects through our digital platform.
